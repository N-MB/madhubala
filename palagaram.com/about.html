About Us

Welcome To Palagaram.com

Palagaram.com is nestled in the most significant cosmic ecstatic energy centre or epicenter of the equator, Chidambaram, Cuddalore District. She has been evolved with an aspiration to cater healthy food with a mix of organic to the citizen of the society to ensure their wellness being balanced.

Behind the scenario, a passionate mans love for food paved a way for this new venture with traditional aroma served with beat taste in a better state of ambiance. Trailing back, the legacy of third generation of Chidambarams most looked upon house hold textile and readymade since 1943 M/s. Kasthuribai Company NMP Readymade, its Chief Executive Officer Mr. V.Muthukumar, aged 44 years, Management Studies by education covered with financial fundamentals known for his excellence and commitment for continuous growth of the business to fulfill the aspirations of his Customers, Employees and Shareholders.

His deep touch and care for well-being awakened Palagarm.com on July 03, 2015. A South Indian Classical Air-conditioned Vegetarian Restaurant with 32 covers purely to quench the people varied taste. The Talk and demand of the people, by the people and for the people boomed – The Multicuisine Air-conditioned Vegetarian Restaurant with 56 covers on October 11, 2016 adding another milestone to its cap.

Enjoy the gastronomical delights proffered at our main restaurant. The eclectic menu across three continents is created to titillate your taste bud. Our main restaurant serves breakfast, lunch and dinner or a light snack accompanied by a variety of beverages on A la carte Basis. The banquet hall Ananda Lahari (Ananda means Bliss; Lahari means Waves) caters up to 120 and can be comfortably customized to suit the occasion private, corporate or other events. The wholesome service, food and attention devoted to ensure your special occasion will ever remain memorable.
